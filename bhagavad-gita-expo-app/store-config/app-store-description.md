## Description (4000 characters max)

Discover the timeless wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita in this beautifully crafted mobile app. Experience sacred Sanskrit verses with comprehensive translations, daily spiritual guidance, and a personalized favorites system.

**Key Features:**

📿 **Daily Shloka** - Receive a different sacred verse each day based on an ancient rotation system, bringing consistent spiritual guidance to your daily practice.

📚 **Complete Chapter Browse** - Explore all 18 chapters of the Bhagavad Gita with their Sanskrit names, English translations, and detailed summaries.

🔍 **Powerful Search** - Find specific verses, concepts, or teachings across Sanskrit text, transliteration, and all translation types with intelligent highlighting.

❤️ **Personal Favorites** - Save meaningful verses to your personal collection for easy access during meditation and reflection.

🕉️ **Authentic Sanskrit** - Read original Devanagari script with accurate transliteration and multiple translation views including word-by-word meanings and detailed commentary.

✨ **Beautiful Design** - Immerse yourself in a spiritually uplifting interface with gradient backgrounds, smooth animations, and respectful presentation of sacred texts.

🌙 **Dark & Light Themes** - Choose between light and dark modes for comfortable reading in any environment.

📱 **Offline Access** - All content is available offline, ensuring your spiritual practice is never interrupted.

♿ **Accessibility** - Full screen reader support, scalable fonts, and high contrast options make the app accessible to all users.

**Perfect for:**

- Daily spiritual practice and meditation
- Students of Hindu philosophy and Sanskrit
- Anyone seeking timeless wisdom and guidance
- Yoga practitioners and spiritual seekers

The Bhagavad Gita, often called the "Song of God," is one of the most revered spiritual texts in the world. This app brings these sacred teachings to your mobile device with the respect and beauty they deserve.

All content is included with the app - no internet connection required after installation. Your favorites and preferences are securely stored on your device.
